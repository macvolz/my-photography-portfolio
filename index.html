About

I'm Ranjan

A street photographer from Assam, India — roaming the lanes, bazaars, and corners that most people walk past without a second glance.

Under the name MACVOLZ, my mission is simple: to prove that millions of stories live on every street, waiting for someone with a camera and the patience to see them.

I shoot raw, honest, and unposed. No studios. No setups. Just life as it happens.
