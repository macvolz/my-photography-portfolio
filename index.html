Hey, I'm Ranjan — a street photographer from Rangia, Assam. Under the name MACVOLZ, I roam the gullys and streets of my hometown, chasing those raw, unscripted moments that most people walk past.

I started this journey less than a year ago with just my phone — an iQOO Neo 10R. No fancy camera, no expensive lenses. Just my eyes and a device in my pocket, capturing the soul of Rangia one frame at a time.

This isn't just photography. It's gully storytelling — authentic, raw, and unapologetically from the heart of Assam.
