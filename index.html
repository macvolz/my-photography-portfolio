About Me

I'm Ranjan

Street photographer from Assam, India — walking under the name MACVOLZ.

I believe the streets of India hold more stories than any book ever written. My lens chases the raw, the real, and the unnoticed — a glance across a crowded bazaar, light breaking through monsoon clouds, the weight of a life lived on a face passing by.

One photo. Millions of stories. That's the goal.
