I'm Ranjan, a street photographer roaming the pulse of India's cities under the name MACVOLZ. My lens chases the decisive moment — the flash of humanity between strangers, the poetry of everyday chaos.

From the monsoon-drenched ghats of Varanasi to the relentless rush of Mumbai's local trains, I document what most eyes walk past. Light, shadow, motion, stillness — these are my tools.

Street photography isn't just what I do. It's how I see the world — one unposed, unfiltered frame at a time.
